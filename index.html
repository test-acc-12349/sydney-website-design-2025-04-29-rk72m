Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your web design packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, 12 months of free hosting, SSL certificate, and unlimited revisions during the design phase. We also provide post-launch support and maintenance.

How long does it take to complete a website?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks. Simple websites can be done in as little as 1 week, while more complex e-commerce sites may take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive maintenance packages that include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support. Our team is available 24/7 for emergency assistance.

What makes your websites SEO-friendly?

We implement advanced SEO best practices including semantic HTML5, optimized meta tags, schema markup, mobile optimization, and fast loading speeds. We also provide detailed SEO reports and recommendations.

Can you help with content creation?

Yes, we offer professional copywriting services to help create engaging, SEO-optimized content for your website. Our team can also assist with image selection, video production, and other content needs.
